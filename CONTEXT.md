# Project Context — CarLabra Estimate Tool

## Business

CarLabra is an auto body repair and car paint workshop located in Espoo, Finland.

The website is not just a basic landing page. It is a lead-generation and price-estimation tool for car paint and body repair services.

## Main goal

Create a modern web-based calculator that allows customers to:

1. Select damaged car parts.
2. Select the damage level.
3. Get an estimated price range.
4. Submit a request.
5. Contact CarLabra through WhatsApp with a pre-filled message.

## Current product type

This is a production-ready MVP for customer acquisition.

It is not yet:
- a full CRM
- a full CABAS replacement
- a backend repair management system
- an AI photo damage recognition product

## Main customer flow

1. Customer enters the website.
2. Customer sees premium CarLabra branding.
3. Customer starts the estimate.
4. Customer chooses one or multiple damaged parts.
5. Customer chooses damage level: Light, Medium, Heavy.
6. Website calculates a price range.
7. Customer fills name and phone.
8. Customer can submit the request and/or open WhatsApp.
9. WhatsApp opens with a clear pre-filled message for the manager.
10. Manager receives structured information and can respond quickly.

## Important business notes

The estimate is always approximate. Final price is confirmed after physical inspection.

The site should feel like a professional automotive tool, not a cheap form.
